Vocabulary of computer interaction may still be nascent

The vocabulary of today's computer systems are rudimentary, there are verbs of this language that we have not discovered yet; verbs that are only in the nascent stages of exploration. Upon these verbs rests the future of computing as a medium.

Expanding the vocabulary of computer interaction is key to expanding the expressiveness of computers as a medium for thinking and communicating.
