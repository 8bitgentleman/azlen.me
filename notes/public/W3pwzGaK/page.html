Applications are designed for authoring not thinking

Computer applications are designed for authoring final products and are not typically adequate as tools for thought. I cannot open Premiere Pro and begin brainstorming ideas for a video to make, instead the video editor comes in the final stage of the process.

Characteristics of a tool for thought:

  • Tools for thought should be flexible in their use
  • Tools for thought enable different workflows
  • Tools for thought should give the user agency
  • Tools for thought should blur the line between users, designers, and programmers
  • Tools for thought are not always intuitive
